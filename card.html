Eastern Gray Squirrel

Squirrel's front teeth never stop growing so they can keep on gnawing and chewing!

  • Scientific Name: Sciurus Carolinensis
  • Average Length: 18 Inches
  • Average Lifespan: 8 Years
  • Habitat: Woodlands and Urban Areas

Squirrels are acrobatic and can jump upto 10 times their body length. They can fall from any height without being hurt. A Baby squirrel is called a Kit and their nests are known as Dreys!
